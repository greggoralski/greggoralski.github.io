Privacy Policy

Welcome to Tennis Battle! We want to be completely transparent about how we handle your information.

No Information is Collected or Tracked

Tennis Battle is a simple, fun game designed purely for entertainment. We do not collect, store, or track any personal information from our users. This includes:

  • Personal data or contact information
  • Gameplay statistics or scores
  • Device information or location data
  • Cookies or tracking technologies
  • Any other user behavior data

The game runs entirely on your device and doesn't connect to any external servers or databases. Your privacy is completely protected because there's simply no mechanism in place to collect any information.

This is a simple game created for fun by a 9-year-old tennis fan and his geek dad. We believe in keeping things simple and respecting your privacy.
